1 head of cauliflower
1 can of chickpeas
1 tbsp of yellow mustard
1 tbsp "bone suckin mustard"
1 tbsp vinegrette 
1/2 cup of olive oil

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
Cut the cauliflower into bit size pieces and rinse the chickpeas. Place in mixing bowl.
Drizzle a quarter cup of olive oil and salt over mixture.
Place in oven and roast for 50 minutes. 

Combine the two mustards, vinagrette, and olive oil in a small mixing bowl.
Whisk until thoroughly mixed.
Drizzle over roasted cauliflower and chickpea after removing from the oven.
